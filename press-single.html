Themes

GamaTravel realised that travellers today have just been going to the same places, taking the same photos, and buying the same souvenirs as everyone else. Trips have turned into packed itineraries, rushing to tick off checklists, and merely recycling holidays in over-tourism spots.

We surprise the world with a difference they've never experienced.

Adventure

The world is your playground, and Mother Nature is its architect. You bring the curiosity and courage.

Test your mental and physical boundaries as you try conquering some of the biggest natural obstacles, whether it's the lushest of forests, highest of cliffs or deepest of oceans. You only have one life, so head out and get lost in the nature’s embrace.

Some of the greatest rewards lie at the end of the toughest trails. GamaTravel kickstarts that journey - from there, it’s up to you to go forth and carve out the adventure of a lifetime!

Experience

Place yourself in the shoes of others to see how they really live - the world is filled with countless pairs for you to try!

Enter the world of villages untouched by external influences, ancient religious complexes that have witnessed years of history, or bustling local markets filled with wares and eats you’ve never seen before! With an abode located in a localised neighbourhood, take a stab at learning their language and make connections with the natives. Your destination also promises to be one that strays from the tourist-beaten path, with locations and activities specially curated for you to immerse in their culture and truly live like a local. Participate in traditions that have lasted for generations, take a trip back in time to understand their history or pick up new skills that might stick with you for life.

Getaway

Lose yourself while trying some laid-back recreational activities, or simply indulge in the moment as you lounge for hours on end, responsibility-free.

When you go on an GamaTravel Getaway trip, you save the hassle of choosing your destination as well as booking your flights and accommodation, so your great escape begins the moment you get off work. We’ll arrange an abode that’s in or nearby an idyllic spot, so rest and recuperation time is just a few footsteps away. You’ll also get insights into the best unspoiled and pristine locations in the area, specially curated for your unwinding needs - whether it’s a romantic holiday or just a quick weekend escape.

Deflated by the mundanity of life? Go on the perfect getaway with GamaTravel, and get your energy levels recharged to a full 100%!
